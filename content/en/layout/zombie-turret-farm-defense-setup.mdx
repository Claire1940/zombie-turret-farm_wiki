<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimal Base Layout Strategies">

A proper **zombie turret farm defense setup** requires more than just high-damage units; it requires a layout that maximizes coverage. As you expand your base, the cost of additional slots increases, making it imperative to use every square inch of your defensive wall effectively.

| Layout Name | Focus Area | Best For | Key Advantage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starter Wall** | Dense grouping | New players | Maximizes early-game kill zones |
| **Balanced Expansion** | Incremental growth | Mid-game | Prevents resource depletion |
| **AFK Fortress** | Peak DPS output | Offline farming | Highest passive income generation |
| **The Funnel** | Overlapping fire | High-speed waves | Prevents zombies from leaking |

For players reaching the mid-game, the "Balanced Expansion Layout" is the most efficient. This involves expanding the base one section at a time. Before purchasing the next expansion, ensure that all current slots are filled with at least A-Tier or B-Tier turrets that have been upgraded at least three times. This ensures that you don't have "dead space" in your base that contributes nothing to your income.

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Mistake">
Avoid buying base expansions if you cannot afford to put a turret in the new slot immediately. An empty expansion is a wasted investment that could have been used for turret upgrades.
</Callout>
