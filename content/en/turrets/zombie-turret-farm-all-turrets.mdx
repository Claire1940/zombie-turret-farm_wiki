<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Initial Wall">
    Fill every available slot on your starting wall. An empty slot is wasted potential. Even a common turret is better than nothing because it contributes to your total income per second.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The 5-Level Rule">
    Before rolling for a new turret, try to upgrade your current best turret to at least Level 5. This provides a guaranteed power spike that is not dependent on the RNG of the rolling system.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Expansion">
    Only purchase a base expansion when your current wall is fully occupied and upgraded. Expanding an empty base does not increase your damage; it only gives you more work to do.
