<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Early Setup Guide">

Establishing a strong defensive wall is your first major milestone. Follow these progression steps to optimize your layout and resource allocation during your first gaming session.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Fill Every Available Wall Slot">
    Roll for basic turrets and place them in every open slot along your starting wall. An occupied slot dealing low damage is always better than an empty slot generating zero income.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Concentrate Upgrades on Key Areas">
    Instead of leveling up all turrets equally, focus your cash on the turrets covering the primary choke points where zombies cluster. This maximizes your active damage output.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Purchase First Base Expansion">
    Once your initial turret line is upgraded to level 5 or higher, buy your first base expansion. This unlocks more slots, allowing you to place additional weapons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Roll for Higher Rarity Units">
    With a larger wall and stable income, start rolling for rarer turrets. Replace your lowest-performing starting units with these stronger options.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Expansion Traps">
