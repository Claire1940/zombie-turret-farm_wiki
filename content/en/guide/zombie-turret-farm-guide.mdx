<Section icon={Shield} title="Best Base Layouts for Maximum Efficiency">

Layout optimization is where advanced players separate themselves from the crowd. In Zombie Turret Farm, the position of your turrets determines how much time they spend firing versus idling. A poorly designed base will have "dead zones" where zombies can pass through without taking damage from all your units simultaneously.

**The "Focus Fire" Strategy:**
The most effective layout involves clustering your strongest turrets at the very front of the zombie lane. This ensures that every enemy is hit by your highest DPS units the moment they spawn. As you expand, you should maintain a continuous line of fire rather than scattered clusters.

<Callout type="warning" title="Layout Mistakes">
Avoid "Gap Expansion." Many players purchase base expansions but leave the new slots empty while they wait for a Legendary roll. It is better to place a Common turret in that slot immediately to contribute to the total damage output.
</Callout>

| Layout Type | Best For | Pros | Cons |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starter Wall** | Level 1-20 | Low cost, easy setup | Limited scaling |
| **Checkerboard** | Mid-Game | Balanced coverage | Harder to manage |
| **Front-Loaded** | Late-Game | Maximum burst damage | Vulnerable to leaks |
| **AFK Hybrid** | Offline Play | Consistent income | Slower active kills |
