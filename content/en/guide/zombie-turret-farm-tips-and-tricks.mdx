<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimal Upgrade Priority">

Knowing what to upgrade first will significantly speed up your progression. Many players make the mistake of expanding their base before they have the firepower to fill it. Follow these steps to ensure you are spending your coins on the most impactful improvements.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Wall Saturation">
    Roll enough basic turrets to fill every available slot in your starting base. An empty slot is wasted potential income.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Damage over Speed">
    Focus on upgrading turret damage first. Higher damage per shot ensures zombies die before they move out of range, which is critical for efficiency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Expansion">
    Only buy base expansions when your current turrets are at a high level. Expansion adds empty slots; you need to be able to afford the turrets to fill them immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Rarity Cycling">
    Once your income is stable, begin replacing your lowest-rarity turrets with new rolls. Do this one slot at a time to maintain consistent damage output.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Upgrade Type | Priority | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Turret Damage** | **Highest** | Decreases time-to-kill and increases cash flow |
| **Attack Speed** | **High** | Essential for handling larger zombie hordes |
| **Base Expansion** | **Medium** | Provides long-term scaling but high upfront cost |
| **Rarity Rolling** | **Variable** | High potential but relies on RNG luck |

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">
Always upgrade your highest-rarity turret first. Because upgrades are percentage-based, they provide a much larger total damage boost to a Legendary turret than to a Common one.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Best Base Layout Strategies">

Your base layout determines how many zombies your turrets can hit at once. A poor layout results in "leaked" zombies that take too long to kill, while a perfect layout creates a meat grinder that generates maximum profit.

One of the best **zombie turret farm tips and tricks** for layouts is the "Concentrated Fire" method. Instead of spreading your best turrets across the entire wall, group them at the beginning of the zombie path. This ensures that most zombies are weakened or killed before they even reach the middle of your base.

| Layout Style | Best For | Key Advantage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Front-Loaded** | Early Game | Kills zombies instantly as they spawn |
| **Staggered Wall** | Mid Game | Ensures consistent damage across the whole path |
| **The Gauntlet** | Late Game | Maximizes total slots for high-rarity units |
| **AFK Setup** | Offline Play | Balanced damage to prevent any leaks during idle |

<Callout type="info" title="Layout Fact">
Zombies have a fixed path. By placing your highest-range turrets in the corners, they can often cover two different sections of the path, effectively doubling their utility.
</Callout>

</Section>
