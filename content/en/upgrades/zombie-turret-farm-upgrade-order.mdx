Success in this Roblox-based simulator depends entirely on how you manage your limited resources. The **zombie turret farm upgrade order** isn't just about clicking the most expensive button; it is about creating a sustainable cycle of income that grows whether you are actively playing or AFK. At its core, the game challenges you to balance three main spending avenues: purchasing new turret rolls, leveling up existing turrets, and expanding your base to hold more units.

| Priority Level | Action Item | Primary Benefit | Resource Target |

| **Strategic** | Rarity Rolling | High-tier power spikes | Excess Surplus |

The initial phase of the game is a race to fill your wall. An empty slot is wasted potential. Even the weakest Common turret is better than an empty space because it contributes to the total kill count, which directly translates to your bank balance.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Walkthrough">

To follow the optimal **zombie turret farm upgrade order**, you must transition through specific developmental phases. Following this sequence prevents the "stagnation trap" where zombies outpace your damage output, leading to slow income rates.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Wall Saturation">
    Your first goal is to ensure every available spot on your starting wall is occupied. Use basic rolls to get any turret available. Do not worry about rarity at this stage; simply focus on having a continuous line of fire against the incoming horde.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Level 10 Threshold">
    Once the wall is full, begin upgrading each turret to Level 10. This level usually provides a significant cost-to-damage ratio benefit. Upgrading is a guaranteed power increase, whereas rolling for new turrets is a gamble that might leave you with a duplicate or a weaker unit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Base Expansion">
    After your initial units are stable, purchase your first base expansion. This opens up new slots. Immediately fill these new slots with rolls. The goal is to maximize the number of projectiles on screen as quickly as possible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Rarity Transitioning">
    With a larger, upgraded wall, you will now have a steady flow of cash. Start replacing your lowest-level Common turrets with Rare or Epic rolls. Only replace a turret once you have a superior one ready to take its place to avoid temporary DPS drops.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Progression Note">
The cost of upgrades scales exponentially. If the cost of the next level for an existing turret exceeds the cost of a new base expansion, always choose the expansion first.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Turret Rarity and Upgrade Efficiency">

Understanding the value of what you are rolling is crucial. The **zombie turret farm upgrade order** changes once you start hitting higher rarities. A Legendary turret requires significantly more investment to level up but offers multipliers that Common units can never reach.

| Rarity Tier | Damage Multiplier | Upgrade Cost | Recommended Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Common** | 1.0x | Very Low | Early game filler / Slot saturation |
| **Rare** | 1.5x | Low | Mid-game backbone |
| **Epic** | 2.5x | Moderate | High-efficiency farming |
| **Legendary** | 5.0x+ | High | Late-game carry / Boss clearing |
| **Mythic** | Massive | Very High | End-game optimization |

When you obtain a high-rarity turret, it should become the focus of your upgrade currency. However, do not neglect the rest of your wall. A "tall" strategy (one super-strong turret) is often less effective than a "wide" strategy (many moderately strong turrets) because of how zombie health pools are distributed across the lane.

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Strategy">
Keep at least two "trash" slots on your wall. These are slots where you place your newest rolls to test them. If they aren't an improvement, you can safely replace them without hurting your core defensive line.
</Callout>

</Section>

Expansion should be viewed as a multiplier for your existing strength. If your current turrets are weak, adding more slots won't help as much as making the existing ones stronger. Conversely, if your turrets are maxed out for your current level, expansion is the only way to continue growing.

| Expansion Phase | Cost Tier | Slot Gain | Priority |
