<Section icon={Map} title="Base Layout and Expansion Tactics">

As you accumulate wealth through **zombie turret farm income upgrades**, you will eventually need to expand your base. Expansion isn't just about adding more turrets; it's about optimizing the "kill zone." A well-organized layout ensures that zombies are under constant fire from the moment they spawn until they are defeated.

- **Concentrated Fire:** Place your highest-level turrets at the start of the lane to weaken high-health zombies immediately.
- **Clean-up Crew:** Use faster-firing, lower-damage turrets at the end of the lane to pick off any stragglers that survived the initial barrage.
- **Expansion Timing:** Only purchase a new expansion when your current wall is fully upgraded. An empty expansion slot is a liability that slows down your overall income growth.

| Layout Strategy | Pros | Cons |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Front-Loading** | Kills zombies fast | Vulnerable to leaks if overwhelmed |
| **Balanced Spread** | Consistent damage | Slower individual kill times |
| **End-Loading** | Catches every straggler | Lower total income efficiency |

<Callout type="tip" title="Layout Tip">
If you find that zombies are frequently reaching the end of your base, don't just add more turrets. Check if your current turrets are targeting the same zombie (overkill). Upgrading fire rate can help spread the damage more effectively.
</Callout>
