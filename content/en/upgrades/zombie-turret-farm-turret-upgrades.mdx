<Section icon={Map} title="Optimal Progression Path">

Navigating the early game to mid-game transition in Zombie Turret Farm can be slow if you misallocate your coins. Following a step-by-step progression route prevents bottlenecks and keeps your income stream flowing smoothly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build the Initial Turret Wall">
    Fill every single available starter slot on your base wall with basic turrets. Do not worry about their rarity at this point; your immediate goal is to establish a continuous line of fire to secure early zombie kills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Strengthen Placed Defenses">
    Invest your initial profits directly into upgrading the damage and speed of the turrets already on your wall. Upgrading existing units provides a guaranteed power increase, whereas rolling for new turrets is random.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Expand the Base Slots">
    Once your active turrets are easily clearing waves, purchase your first base expansion. This adds permanent physical slots to your wall, allowing you to deploy more turrets simultaneously.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Roll for High-Rarity Replacements">
    With a stable economy and a larger wall, begin rolling for rarer turrets. When you secure a high-rarity roll, place it in your most valuable wall slot and focus your upgrades on it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Progression Milestone">
Once you have unlocked at least two base expansions and filled them with upgraded Rare or Epic turrets, your passive income generation will skyrocket, making future upgrades significantly easier to purchase.
</Callout>
